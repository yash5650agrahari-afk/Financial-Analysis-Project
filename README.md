# Financial-Analysis-Project
A Python-based financial data analysis project where I explored sales and profit trends across countries, products, and customer segments using a real-world dataset.

---

## Why I Built This

During my internship in the Finance Department at GAIL, I realized how important it is to quickly make sense of large financial datasets. This project is my attempt to apply that thinking using Python — cleaning raw data, finding patterns, and presenting insights clearly.

---

## Tools & Libraries

- Python
- Pandas — data cleaning and analysis
- Matplotlib & Seaborn — visualizations
- Openpyxl — Excel report generation

---

## Dataset

- Source: Kaggle — Financial Sample Dataset (Microsoft)
- 700 rows, 16 columns
- Fields: Segment, Country, Product, Sales, Profit, COGS, Date, Year

---

## What I Analyzed

- Total Sales and Profit overview
- Sales performance by Country
- Profit breakdown by Product
- Profit contribution by Customer Segment
- Year-on-year Sales trend

---

## Key Insights

- Total Revenue: **$118.7 Million** | Total Profit: **$17.7 Million**
- **USA** was the top performing country with $25M in sales
- **Paseo** was the most profitable product at $4.9M
- **Government segment** contributed 64% of total profit
- Sales grew **3.5x from 2013 to 2014**
